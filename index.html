What's included?

Each project is tailored to your specific needs and can include wireframes, design specifications, user research, interactive prototypes, requirements documentation, competitive analysis, and more. We work together to define the deliverables that will have the greatest impact on your business.

Should I choose the day rate or a monthly retainer?

If your project is short-term or has a very specific goal, the day rate is ideal for achieving focused results. For evolving projects that need regular input and continuous design iterations, the monthly retainer is more cost-effective and provides consistent access to expertise over time.

Can I switch from the day rate to a monthly retainer?

Absolutely! As your needs grow, you can transition to a retainer to ensure ongoing access to the expertise and services required for larger, more complex projects.
